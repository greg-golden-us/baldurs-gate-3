# Vane — Leveling & Equipment Guides

This file contains per-character leveling paths, key features, and essential gear to follow as the party advances. The builds are derived from Core Party Strategy and Honor-Mode notes.

## Karlach — The Throwing Berserker
Final Build: Barbarian 5 / Rogue (Thief) 3 / Fighter (Champion) 3

Levels 1–4: Barbarian 1–4
- Feat: Tavern Brawler (+1 CON)
- Essential Gear: Returning Pike, Ring of Flinging, Gloves of Uninhibited Kushigo

Levels 5–8: Barbarian 5, Rogue 1–3
- Extra Attack at L5. Thief for bonus action utility.
- Gear: Caustic Band, Returning Pike

Levels 9–12: Rogue 4, Fighter 1–3
- Feat: Alert
- Champion for expanded crit range (19–20)
- Gear goals: Dwarven Thrower or similar heavy-hitting throw weapon

---

## Astarion — The Open Hand Monk
Final Build: Monk (Open Hand) 9 / Rogue (Thief) 3

Levels 1–4: Monk 1–4
- Feat: Tavern Brawler (+1 CON)
- Gear: Bracers of Defence, Sparkle Hands

Levels 5–8: Monk 5–8
- Gain Extra Attack & Stunning Strike at Lv5
- ASI into Wisdom
- Gear: Sentient Amulet (Ki sustain), Gloves of Cinder

Levels 9–12: Respec path to Monk 6 / Thief 3 for Fast Hands and enhanced bonus action economy.

---

## Shadowheart — The Radiant Orb Cleric
Final Build: Light Domain Cleric (12)

Levels 1–4: Light Cleric 1–4
- Radiance of the Dawn, War Caster
- Gear: Blood of Lathander (mace), Luminous Armour

Levels 5–8: Light Cleric 5–8
- Spirit Guardians, Alert feat recommended
- Gear: Luminous Gloves, Coruscation Ring, Haste Helm

Levels 9–12: Focus on Destructive Wave, ASI to Wisdom.

---

## Vane (Main) — College of Lore Bard / Warlock Split
Final Build: Lore Bard 10 / Warlock 2 (example goal)

Levels 1–5: Lore Bard 1–5
- Cutting Words, Expertise in social skills
- Gear: Phalar Aluve, Melf's First Staff

Levels 6–8: Lore Bard 6 / Warlock 1–2
- Magical Secrets: Counterspell & Haste
- Invocations: Agonizing/Repelling Blast
- Gear: Potent Robe, Spineshudder Amulet, Adamantine Shield

Levels 9–12: Continue Magical Secrets, ASI into Charisma.
