Core Party Strategy

* Elixir Strategy: Set Strength to 8 for Karlach and Astarion. Purchase Elixirs of Hill Giant Strength from Auntie Ethel in the Emerald Grove. She stocks 3 per long rest (or per level-up).  
* The Face: The Lore Bard handles all conversations, lockpicking, and counter-spelling.  
* The Combo: Shadowheart applies Radiating Orb to make enemies miss, while Karlach and Astarion eliminate priority targets.
